Reborn to Master the Blade: From Hero-King to Extraordinary Squire ♀

With the Goddess’ blessing, he became a 『Divine Knight』 and founded an Empire. As time passed, he grows older, and by the time the heavens called for him to come, the Goddess showed before him, asking for his last wish. He wished to be reincarnated in the future. He dedicated all his life for his people, he had no time to master his own arts. This time, he wishes to live for himself, forging his body to the utmost limit. The Goddess complied with his wish. In the far future, the King shall reincarnate as the 『daughter』 of a renowned Knight family. And, under some circumstances, she is labeled as a failure of a Knight, however—— 「How convenient. I can stand on the foremost line without needing any rank. No better training than actual combat!」 Thus, the legend of the world’s strongest, most beautiful Knight Disciple (♀) has begun—
